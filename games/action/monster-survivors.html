About Monster Survivors

Monster Survivors is an action-packed survival game where your goal is simple yet challenging: survive as long as possible against endless waves of monsters. As time progresses, monsters become stronger and more numerous, testing your skills and strategy.

In this top-down adventure, you'll navigate through a dynamically changing world, collecting experience points to level up and enhance your character's abilities. The longer you survive, the more powerful you become - but so do your enemies!

What sets Monster Survivors apart is its roguelike elements combined with action gameplay. Each run is unique, with random power-ups and enemy patterns creating a fresh experience every time you play. Can you discover the optimal build and strategy to achieve the highest survival time?

How to Play

The controls are simple and intuitive:

  • Use WASD keys to move your character around the map
  • Your character attacks automatically in the direction of nearby enemies
  • Collect experience gems (green) to level up
  • When you level up, choose from one of three random upgrades
  • Pick up health items (red) to restore your HP
  • Try to survive as long as possible!

Tips & Strategies

  • Keep moving! Standing still is a quick way to get surrounded by monsters. Constantly reposition yourself to avoid being cornered.
  • Prioritize your upgrades. Focus on a few powerful abilities rather than spreading your upgrades too thin. Specializing in complementary powers creates powerful synergies.
  • Collect all experience gems. These power your level-ups which are crucial for survival as the game progresses and enemies get stronger.
  • Watch for special events. Bosses can appear after surviving certain time thresholds. Be prepared for sudden difficulty spikes.
  • Balance offense and defense. While offensive upgrades help clear enemies faster, don't neglect defensive options like increased health or regeneration.
